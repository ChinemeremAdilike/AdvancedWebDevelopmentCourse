I got a clearer idea of how JavaScript handles numbers and calculations, and I like how simple many of the math operations are.

## 11. Arrays
I learned how arrays let me store multiple items in a single place, and they seem super useful for organizing lists of data.

I learned how objects can store related pieces of data, and JSON stood out to me because it's used so often for sending information between applications.
